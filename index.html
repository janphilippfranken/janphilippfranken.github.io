Naïve information aggregation in human social learning

Jan-Philipp Fränken, Simon Valentin, Christopher Lucas, Neil Bramley

We compared human inferences with an idealised rational account that corrects for correlated belief reports by evaluating peers' communications with respect to the underlying communication pathway.
